Weight Loss



  • Intermittent fasting
    • 16-8 fast (skip breakfast and eat at 12)
    • Omad: eat one meal a day
    • Fast every Other day for 24hrs
  • Keto Diet
    • Limit carbohydrates to less than 20g per day
    • No added sugars, bread, pasta, fruits, candy
    • Stick to meats and vegetables
  • Carnivore Diet
    • Eat only meat
    • Stick for grass fed beaf, organ meat, fish, water and salt
  • Exercice
    • Cardio| HIIT: High intensity interval training (sprinting for 30 sec rest for 40 *10)
    • Resistance Training| Lift weights (Stick to compound lifts: Squats, Deadlifts, Bench Press, etc)
  • Sleep
    • At least 7-9 hours a sleep a night for muscle resting
    • Helps your metabolism
    • Do not expose eyes to Blue light at least 3 hrs before sleep

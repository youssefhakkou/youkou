Longevity



  • Diet
    • Eat a Paleo diet composed of unprocessed meat and vegetables
    • Avoid food that our ancestors would not have eaten
  • Sleep
    • Avoid Blue Light
    • Avoid cafeinated beverages 5 hrs before sleep
  • Showers
    • Take cold showers for an anti-anflammatory effect on the body
    • Use natural soaps and deoderant
  • Supplements
    • Vitamin D3 and Omega 3 Fatty Acids are important anti-inflammatories
  • Family and Friends
    • Surround yourself with people who support at all times
    • Avoid toxic relationships, family and friends
  • Saunas and Chryotherapy
    • Try to go into a sauna at least twice a week for the heat shock proteins (hormesis: good stress on the body)
    • Try to expose yourself to cold water or go in a chryochamber for the cold shock proteins
  • Educational Podcasts
    • Keep the mind active with educational material on health (listen to Joe Rogan podcast, Dr Rhonda Patrick)
    • Listen to philosophical material (like School of Life and Dr. Jordan Peterson)
  • Mindfullness
    • Meditate once a day for a least 15min to keep the mind calm
    • Practice a least 15min of yoga a day for flexibility
  • Hobbies
    • Master a hobby
    • Keep busy to avoid existential dread
